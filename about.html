About me

Hello... I am grateful that I was born, on September 16 1998, into this world. I am grateful that I am the fourth child of my parents. I am grateful that I spent my childhood life in Kebumen, a small city in Indonesia with a lot of beautiful landscapes, people, and cultures. I am grateful that I was given the opportunity to study engineering physics in Bandung Institute of Technology (ITB), an educational institution with a lot of amazing people and ideas. I am grateful that I am able to experience life as Angga Bayu Prakhosha.

In this life, I am interested in a lot of things. From science and philosophy to pop culture and entertainment. And because of this, at first I honestly thought that I do not have any reason to live for myself. But as time goes on, I am able to find many interesting thing in this world, or what people would called as hobby. I like to play games, I like RPG games and I like games where I can be immersed by it. I like having to read something, whether its fiction or non-fiction. If it is fiction, it has to be told in the first-person point of view. I expressed my admiration for great thinkers such as Wittgenstein, Buddha, Ki Hadjar Dewantara, and many more.

My goal in this life is to fully experience this life and live a contended life, a life where I won't regret any choices that I made in this life at the last day of my life.
